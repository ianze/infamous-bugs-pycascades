Ariane 5 Explosion

The rocket was carrying 4 satellites. Designed to for a constellation
Key in exploring earth magnetic field, interaction with sun and space weather
June 4th 1996 - failed (37)40 seconds into launch.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gp_D8r-2hwk

Overflow

Countdown postponed and resumed too fast. IRS - Inertial Reference System This Diagnosis subroutine running during flight, but shouldn't have and wouldn't on the model4.

Overflow due to high altitude. In the ada code we see here (red variable)
Ada code. 64-bit floating point number to a 16-bit signed integer
4 out of 7 variables protected from overflow

Both IRS1 & 2 failed same way.

The Results

Total Cost of $370M (rocket and cluster satellites)
Setback of years(4) for the cluster mission
Hurt ESA reputation for successful launches - 2007 - back to same rate
July 16, 2000 - launched by Russia
Large scale static analysis.

Why This Bug

Reuse of a software package(it worked very well on the model 4)

Having a backup system does not work if both fail in the same way.

AT&T Long Distance

Alexander Bell placing the first call between new york and chicago.
January 15 1990 no long distance phone calls at AT&T
For 9 hours engineers working to resolve. Even with law enforcement

Call Waiting...

114 computer-operated electronic switches (4ESS) - First switch (in NY) congested.
Once back in operation the switch started sending the messages in queue.
Handling these messages by other switches was the improved code - two messages too fast
Second switch goes down - cascading effect

The Results

$60M in long distance calls
9 hours outage time
75M phone calls
200,000 airline reservations
Brand/PR damage - AT&T build it's brand as reliable system.
The whole system was designed for reliability - components would monitor health of other components
There was no way to recover from all components having such an issue.

Why This Bug

Improving component performance can lead to adverse effect
Consider each change on the whole system

Patriot System Failure

surface-to-air missile - Patriot used since the 80s. 90s - first time against scud missiles.
Deployed to several allies in the middle east. Including Israel and Saudi Arabia.
On February 25th 1991 a scud missile that has been fired at a US Air base in Daharan.

Failure At Dahhran

Very left explain how system works - then operations after 8 hours - and after 20 - series of radar pulses
System relies on time since it started - store time up to 1/10 in 24 bit register. -
We loose precision and since a routine that was making more accurate only in some parts in code

Daharan system 100 hours, 8 hours - 20%. 20 hours - unusable

The Results

Direct hit on the barracks
28 killed
110 injured
Mainly reserve duty personal from Pennsylvania.
It help to spark a debate over the system accuracy.

Why This Bug

The issue reported (two weeks before) but not prioritized
A gap between how the system is used and how it was assumed to be used
A fix was delivered to the army the next day
